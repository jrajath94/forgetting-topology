\section{Reproducibility}
\label{app:repro}

\paragraph{Code and data.} All code, configurations, and per-cell result JSON files are provided as anonymised supplementary material for peer review, and will be released under the MIT licence at a permanent URL upon acceptance. Datasets are publicly distributed under their original licences (CoNLL-2003, SQuAD-v1.1, XSum, CodeAlpaca-20k, HumanEval). The CoNLL-2003 source used is the parquet mirror \texttt{tomaarsen/conll2003} on the Hugging Face Hub, which has schema identical to the canonical \texttt{conll2003} release.

\paragraph{Hardware.} NVIDIA A40 48GB GPUs on a commercial GPU cloud. bf16 mixed precision, gradient checkpointing enabled.

\paragraph{Software.} Python 3.11, PyTorch 2.4.1 + CUDA 12.4, \texttt{transformers}$\ge$5.5.0, \texttt{peft}$\ge$0.19.0, \texttt{trl}$\ge$1.1.0, \texttt{datasets}$\ge$4.0.0, \texttt{accelerate}$\ge$1.1.1. Exact pinned versions in \texttt{code/requirements.txt} in the supplement.

\paragraph{Seeds.} $\{42,\,1337,\,2024\}$. All tables report mean $\pm$ standard deviation over seeds.

\paragraph{Statistical tests.} Paired $t$-test across seeds for forgetting reductions; Wilcoxon signed-rank as a non-parametric fallback. Spearman's $\rho$ for \fvs{} transferability; Pearson $r$ reported in the appendix for sensitivity. Significance threshold $p<0.05$ unless stated.

Estimated total: \pend{carbon_kgco2e} kgCO$_2$e.
